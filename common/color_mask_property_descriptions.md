### ColorMask
***Koikatsu property***. Color mask of Koikatsu, `Color` - `red`, `Color2` - `green`, `Color3` - `blue`, `blue` > `green` > `red`.

### Color
***Koikatsu property***. The color of the `red` channel of `ColorMask`.           

### Color2
***Koikatsu property***. The color of the `green` channel of `ColorMask`.          

### Color3
***Koikatsu property***. The color of the `blue` channel of `ColorMask`.
